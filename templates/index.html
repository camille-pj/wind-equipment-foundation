When to use each basis (effective height z, §3.1.5.2.2 — the height at which the wind is evaluated):
  • Tip / top (z = ztop): most conservative; the single highest Kz over the element — default.
  • Centroid (z = zbase + L/2): Kz at the element's mid-height; appropriate when the wind force acts at the centroid of a uniform projected area.
  • Custom: evaluate at a stated reference height (e.g. a base plinth taken at the governing equipment height).
